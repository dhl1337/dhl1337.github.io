WAITR

ABOUT

Waitr is a mobile application created by a group of 5 developers. It allows customers to conveniently add their name on a waiting list without being at the restaurant and receive text notification when the table is ready. Customers and restaurant employees can see the waiting list, therefore employers will know whom to seat next and customers know their spot in line. In addition, customers can see the menu, contact information, and store hours.

RESPONSIBILITY

I helped my team carefully plan out each step for the project. Once we got all our ideas together I completed a mock up on sketch then presented it to the group. After everyone has agreed we divided the work. I started from the backend and connect the frontend to the back. If there were any questions, comments, or concerns about the project I would address it.

TECHNOLOGY

The technology used to build Waitr were JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3, AngularJS, MongooseJS, Express.js, Node.js, Socket.io, Ionic, and Moment.js
